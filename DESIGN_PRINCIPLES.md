# Design Principles

## Desired Feeling

The app should feel:

* Calm
* Intentional
* Reflective
* Warm
* Similar level of polish to Apple native apps

The app should NOT feel:

* Retro gimmick
* Toy camera
* Wedding planner
* Social media feed
* Gaming app

## Visual Inspiration

References:

* Apple Journal
* Apple Photos
* Apple Health
* Traveler's Notebook
* Fujifilm packaging
* Japanese seasonal journals

## Colors

Primary palette:

* Warm brown
* Forest green
* Soft cream
* Charcoal

Avoid:

* Pure black
* Neon colors
* Bright gradients

## Typography

Large typography.

Breathing room.

Minimal text.

Important information should feel editorial rather than technical.

Example:

Good:
"Summer Roll"

Bad:
"Roll ID #381"

## Navigation

Few screens.

Few buttons.

One primary action per screen.

## Roll Experience

Rolls should feel like physical objects.

Example:

Summer Roll

12 / 24 exposures

Develops in 8 days

rather than

Event Dashboard

Photos Used: 12

## Camera Experience

The camera should feel deliberate.

Not playful.

Not cluttered.

Viewfinder may be smaller than the screen, but should remain clear and usable.

Uncertainty should come from the reveal, not from poor usability.
